My Experience

Tadika Arifmurni Durian Tunggal, Melaka

After I finished my high school, I started working while waiting for my
SPM results at Tadika Arifmurni Durian Tunggal for 3 months. There
I learned various things in terms of responsibility, friendship and
difficulties in finding money. Not forgetting the very valuable
experience I got while working there. I have considered them like
sisters because they have taught me a lot about life and also they
treat me like their own sisters. Even though we only knew each other
for a short period of time, they never forgot about me and sometimes
asked how I was doing and never forgot that I never forgot to rebuke them.

Library of SK Bukit Beruang

After I took my diploma at UiTM Rembau, I continued my studies for my
degree in Library Management. The picture above is me with the
teacher who manages the library as well as the library supervisor
at the school. Here I can learn various things about the library
and I am currently just helping the teacher who is a friend of
my mother. I really enjoyed this experience and I was also able
to recall my memories at that school since it was my primary
school. I was able to find out how to catalog books, organize
books according to the call number that has been made and also
according to its own section.
